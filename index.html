About me

Originally from Barcelona, I am a creative mind, always trying to navigate between tech, design and art. I am a musician by heart, with a large experience in bands, studio work, composing, producing, editing and mixing, and therefore I am used to be involved in every single part of a creative process. In a similar way, I have brought to UX/UI field this same vision, trying to focus in every detail from the initial research to the final delivery of a product designing.

Back in Barcelona, though, I was working as a social worker, and I am keen to go to the social field. Maybe because of it I am prone to empathise with people, and understand their needs.
